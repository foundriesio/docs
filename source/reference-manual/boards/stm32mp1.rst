Flashing
--------

Now, flash the ``lmp-factory-image-intel-corei7-64.wic.gz`` retrieved from the previous section
to an USB stick. This contains the :term:`system image` installer.

.. include:: generic-flashing.rst

1. Remove the micro SDCard from the host and insert it into the socket of the board.

2. Power on the board to boot the new image.
